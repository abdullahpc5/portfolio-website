Courses I Completed in 2024

In 2024, I expanded my AI and ML knowledge through several comprehensive courses. To complement my technical skills, I completed IBM's Introduction to Project Management, essential for leading and managing AI projects. I also took Google's Introduction to Image Generation, exploring techniques and models for creating synthetic images, and Introduction to Large Language Models, gaining insights into leveraging these models for NLP tasks.
